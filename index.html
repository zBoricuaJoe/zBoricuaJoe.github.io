Who Am I?

Hey! I’m Joe — Caribbean soul, lifelong competitor, storyteller, and digital creator. Born in Bridgeport and moved to Tampa at 16. My journey started at age 5 with karate (earned the legendary white belt before retiring undefeated… in spirit).

Since then I’ve explored sports, gaming, and creative arts with the same energy: go all-in, have fun, make a name. From baseball trophies to basketball street cred, and from MW2 nuke streaks to Halo 2 tournaments, I’ve always been about the grind. Now I stream, game, and create — with my girl by my side since 2008 and my dog Blue holding the record for highest watch time since before Twitch existed.

On TikTok I racked up millions of views dropping 20-bombs and comedic takes; on Twitch I go live with the same energy, ready to frag, troll, and hype up the chat.
